Discover Saint Louis

The site aims to provide a comprehensive guide to the history, culture, and attractions of Saint Louis, Missouri.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to offer detailed information on Saint Louis's historical landmarks, cultural events, local cuisine, and must-visit attractions. It will include sections on popular tourist destinations like the Gateway Arch, Forest Park, and the Saint Louis Zoo, as well as practical travel tips and restaurant recommendations.

Scenarios

  • A tourist planning a visit to Saint Louis wants to find information on the top attractions and historical landmarks to include in their itinerary.
  • A local resident is looking for upcoming cultural events and new restaurants to explore in the city.

Color Schema

Colors:

  • Blue (#1E90FF) for headings and buttons to represent the Mississippi River and the city's vibrant energy.
  • White (#FFFFFF) for the background to create a clean and modern look.

Application: Blue will be used for all headings, buttons, and links. White will be used for the background of the site.

Typography

Font: "Open Sans"

Application: The "Open Sans" font will be used for all text on the site, including headings, body text, and buttons. It is chosen for its readability and modern appearance.

Wireframe

A wireframe for the home page includes:

  • Mobile View: A simplified layout with a hamburger menu, a prominent search bar, featured articles, and quick links to popular sections.
  • Wider View: A more detailed layout with a top navigation bar, a larger search bar, featured articles, quick links, and a sidebar with recent posts and categories.
